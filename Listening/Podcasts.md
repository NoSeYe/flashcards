# IELTS Recommended Podcasts

To improve your listening score (targeting Band 7.0+), use these categorized podcasts.

## 1. IELTS Strategy (Learn the Exam)
- **IELTS Energy English 7+:** Best for specific test strategies and scoring criteria.
- **My IELTS Classroom Podcast:** Deep dives into complex sections, hosted by former examiners.
- **IELTS Speaking for Success:** Natural dialogue practice, good for Section 1 and 3.

## 2. General Skill Building (Level A2 - B2)
- **BBC 6 Minute English:** Perfect for daily habit building and topical vocabulary.
- **SEND7 (Simple English News Daily):** 7-minute daily news summary in intermediate English.
- **English Learning for Curious Minds:** Fascinating topics (science, history) at a slightly slower pace.

## 3. Academic & Advanced Immersion (Band 7.5 - 9.0)
- **TED Radio Hour:** Excellent for Section 4 (Academic Monologues).
- **99% Invisible:** Great for technical descriptions and variety of accents.
- **In Our Time (BBC):** High-level discussions on philosophy, science, and history.

## How to Practice
1. **Active Listening:** Take notes or summarize the episode in 3 sentences after listening.
2. **Shadowing:** Repeat phrases out loud to improve your own speaking and pronunciation.
3. **Transcript Check:** If you miss a word, check the podcast's website for a transcript.
