# IELTS 7.0 Preparation: Phase 1 Daily Routine (Months 1-2)
*Goal: Building Habits & Vocabulary Foundation*

| Time | Activity | Detail |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Morning (15-20m)** | **Vocabulary Input** | Use your **Flashcards App**. Review 20 old words and learn 20 new words. |
| **Commute/Gap (20m)** | **Passive Listening** | Listen to *BBC 6 Minute English* or *The Daily* (New York Times). Don't stress about every word, just get used to the accent. |
| **Lunch Break (15m)** | **Reading Sprint** | Read 1 article from *BBC News* (Science or Technology section). Highlight 3 words you didn't know. |
| **Evening (30m)** | **Active Writing** | Write a "TA Journal entry" (150-200 words). Describe your day at the University using at least 5 words from your flashcards. |
| **Before Bed (10m)** | **Self-Correction** | Read your journal entry out loud. If a sentence sounds "clunky," try to rewrite it. |

---

## Weekly Weekend Tasks
*   **Saturday Morning:** Complete **one** Reading passage from a Cambridge IELTS book (Limit: 20 minutes).
*   **Sunday Evening:** Watch one TED Talk (Academic topic) and try to summarize it in 3 sentences.
